Operational Architecture

  1. Asset Onboarding: A property owner engages PropChain to tokenize an asset.
  2. SPV Formation: A Lebanese Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) is established.
  3. Token Issuance: The PropChain ADGM entity issues digital tokens representing fractional shares.
  4. Primary Sale & Trading: Tokens are sold and then traded on our secondary market.
  5. Revenue & Management: PropChain earns fees; a third-party facility manager handles the property.
